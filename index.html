About Me

Hi there! I'm Ray, a frontend developer based in London. I'm passionate about building beautiful, functional and responsive websites that provide a great user experience. I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to expand my skillset.

What I Can Offer

Whether you need a website for your business, a personal portfolio, or a game, I can help you build it. I can provide web design and development services tailored to your specific needs. From creating a visually appealing and responsive website layout to coding the functionality and interactivity, I can work with you to bring your vision to life.

Modular Code

Clean, maintainable, and reusable code that can be easily integrated into existing projects and extended to meet future requirements. Our code is organized into modular components, making it easier to understand, modify, and reuse.

Responsive Design

Ensure that your website is accessible and usable on all devices, from smartphones to large desktop screens. Our responsive design approach ensures that your website adjusts to different screen sizes and formats, providing an optimal user experience for all users.

Cross-Browser Compatibility

Ensure that your website works correctly on all major browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Our cross-browser compatibility approach ensures that your website displays correctly and functions as expected across all major browsers.

Optimized User Interfaces

Create user interfaces that are intuitive, fast, and engaging, with a focus on usability and accessibility. Our approach involves a thorough understanding of user needs and preferences, as well as a focus on creating intuitive and user-friendly interfaces.
